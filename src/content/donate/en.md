Thank you for your interest in supporting our peace initiative. Your contribution enables Vedic Pandits in India to continue their daily service for world peace.

Scientific research has shown for decades that collective meditation programmes have measurable effects on societal wellbeing — from reduced crime rates to economic stability in affected regions. Funding these programmes is therefore not just a spiritual contribution, but a concrete investment in a more peaceful world.

Every contribution counts — from a one-time gift to monthly sponsorship. We use donations exclusively to support the Pandit programmes; the board of the Peace Initiative works on a volunteer basis.
